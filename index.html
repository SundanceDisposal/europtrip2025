Paris

The City of Light. Your first two days set the tone for everything.

You're starting in arguably the greatest city in the world for art and atmosphere. The Louvre, the Orsay, the Eiffel Tower at dusk, and a full day at Versailles — Paris is where Avery first realizes this trip is unlike anything she's done before.

May 28 – 30  ·  2 nights

Salzburg

Austria's most beautiful small city. Mozart's birthplace. The Alps right outside.

This is the experiential heart of the trip. Salt mines underground. A medieval fortress above. High tea at the most famous café in Austria. An evening concert in a Baroque church. Salzburg is small, totally walkable, and impossible to do wrong.

Salt mines Medieval fortress High tea at Sacher Evening concert Sound of Music
May 29 morning · Book ahead
Hallstatt Salt Mines
Miners' gear, a wooden slide underground, a lamp-lit subterranean lake. Unlike anything else on this continent. She will talk about this for years.
salzwelten.at →
May 29 afternoon
Hohensalzburg Fortress
One of the best-preserved medieval fortresses in Europe. Funicular up, panoramic Alpine views, and the entire old town spread below.
salzburg-burgen.at →
May 29 evening
Café Sacher High Tea
The most iconic café in Austria. Sachertorte, fine tea, Avery in a dress. This is exactly the kind of refined, beautiful moment she'll tell people about.
sacher.com →
May 29 evening
Evening concert
Mozart or classical music performed in a Baroque church or the Mozarteum. Intimate, no language barrier, and genuinely atmospheric.
salzburg-concerts.com →

Vienna

Imperial grandeur. Klimt's The Kiss. White horses in a Baroque hall.

Vienna is where the trip reaches its peak of elegance. Two palaces, The Kiss by Klimt in person, the Spanish Riding School, and possibly a night at the Vienna State Opera. This is the city where Avery gets to feel like she's living in a movie. She should be in a dress the entire time.

Schönbrunn Palace Klimt's The Kiss Spanish Riding School Vienna State Opera High tea
May 31 morning
Schönbrunn Palace
Habsburg summer palace, 40 opulent rooms, and the Gloriette hilltop with the best free view in Vienna. Different from Versailles — more personal, more intimate.
schoenbrunn.at →
May 31 afternoon · Book timed entry
Klimt's "The Kiss" at the Belvedere
One of the most famous paintings in the world — two figures wrapped in gold, lost in a moment. Avery will stand in front of this and feel something. Promise.
belvedere.at →
Jun 1 morning · Book early
Spanish Riding School
White Lipizzaner stallions performing classical dressage in a Baroque imperial hall. She will not expect to be moved by this. She will be moved by this.
srs.at →
May 31 evening · Check schedule
Vienna State Opera
Standing room tickets ~€10, sold 80 minutes before curtain. Even one act in that hall is an experience unlike any other. Check the June schedule now.
wiener-staatsoper.at →

Jun 1 – 4  ·  2 nights + overnight train home

Prague

The most cinematic city in Europe. Medieval, golden, impossibly beautiful.

Prague is where the trip exhales. It's cheaper than everywhere else, your friend's family might be here, and it looks like a fairy tale at every hour of the day. The castle, Charles Bridge, black light theatre, and a relaxed final day — then the overnight train takes you to sleep in Prague and wake up in Paris.

Prague Castle Charles Bridge Black light theatre Local connection Overnight train home
Jun 2 morning
Prague Castle
Largest ancient castle complex in the world. St. Vitus Cathedral, Golden Lane's tiny medieval cottages, and the most beautiful urban panorama in Central Europe.
hrad.cz →
Jun 2 afternoon
Charles Bridge
Baroque statues, musicians, the castle above, the river below. The most romantic bridge in Europe. She will want to linger here.

Jun 2 evening
Black Light Theatre
Abstract visual theatre unique to Prague. Glowing costumes, surreal storytelling, no language needed. Unlike anything else in the world — totally brag-worthy.

Vrtba Garden + spa morning
A hidden Baroque terraced garden most tourists never find. Or a spa morning. Or just a café and a book. This is the Netflix and chill day — totally deserved.
